Michelle Hong (President)

Michelle Christina Hong is a 2-time Jr. National Competitor, a 3-time Sectional Competitor, and a 2-time US National alternate. She competes at the Senior level and has been a Professional Figure Skating Instructor since the age of 16 years old. Currently, she is pursuing a double major in Media Studies and Business Administration at the Haas School of Business with a passion for entrepreneurship. This past year, Michelle was recognized as one of the top 100 young future entrepreneurs in the world by The Summit Dublin and is currently working on a new social media app called Hype-It with a few of her UC Berkeley peers.

Amy Nguyen (Vice-President)

Amy is Junior studying Business Administration at the Haas School of Business who skates at the Juvenile / Intermediate Level. Her favorite skating moves are axels and sit spins. Outside the rink, you can find reading good book or doing yoga.

Matej Silecky (Team Captain)

Matej is the 2012 US Figure Skating Collegiate Junior Men's Champion, and currently competes Senior Men and Senior Pairs. He holds a US Figure Skating Judge appointment and is a registered coach (CER A). At Cal, Matej is an Interdisciplinary Studies major, concentrating on Landscape Architecture, Film and Russian Studies, with a Theatre/Dance minor. He is also active in Cal's E-sports team, snowboarding and NSLS.

Aditya Nandy (Treasurer; Webmaster; Back-up Safety Officer)

Aditya Nandy is a 1-time National, 2-time Jr. National Competitor, a 4-time Sectional Competitor in Juvenile, Intermediate, and Novice Pairs (with partner Ananya Nandy). He is pursuing a major in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering and a minor in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. Outside of school and skating, Aditya likes to hang out with friends, go to Tau Beta Pi events, plan overnight host programs for prospective freshmen, or drink boba. After college, he would like to become a Professor. Together with Audrey Phone and Audrey Tan, Aditya Nandy is part of the comedy trio titled Auditya.
