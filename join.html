Steps to Join Cal Figure Skating


  1. Get an RSF (Recreational Sports Facility) Membership. You MUST be an RSF member to join Cal Figure Skating. Click here to get an RSF membership.

  2. Decide whether or not you would like to compete for the team. There are two teams, a recreational team and a competition team. The competition team travels. The recreational team does not.

  3. Join the team roster by clicking here and going down to the "Figure Skating" section. The roster is the item titled Figure Skating Club Sign Up. The cost is $0.00, but every member MUST "add" this item to their cart.

  4. If on the competition team, scroll down to "Competition Team Dues." Dues for this semester are $55.00. Please be prompt in your payments. We will check them. In order to compete, you will need to have a USFSA number. Obtain a USFSA membership if you do not already have one.

  5. If on the recreational team, scroll down to "Recreational Team Dues." Dues for this semester are $40.00. Please be prompt in your payments. We will check them.

  6. Once you have paid and added yourself to the roster, you have signed up! Please attend our next team meeting, scheduled for January 21, 2015.

  7. If you have any questions or concerns about payment, feel free to contact our treasurer, Aditya, at aditya.nandy@berkeley.edu.
